Who Are We?

Makrr is a software development company located in Palo Alto, California. The differentiator between us and other software development companies is we get inside industries to analyze ways technology can best be integrated. We then go on to design and develop these applications. We only design solutions that fit into an industry. We believe technology is not effective when forced on an industry; instead, we believe it needs to fit in naturally.

What do we do?

Makrr creates innovative solutions for today's problems. We focus on specific industries and find ways technology could naturally fit in to help that industry focus on the important part of their work. We do this by first surveying the industry by doing research, reviewing statistics, and conducing interviews. After this, we develop a prototype of a product that addresses one of the issues brought up by the survey. We then continue to work with the people of the industry, to revise the product to best meet their needs. Once this is done, we release the product to the public.

How Long Have We Been Around?

Makrr was started in early 2007. We started by solely doing iOS development. Today we do web, Android, iPhone, and iPad development.
